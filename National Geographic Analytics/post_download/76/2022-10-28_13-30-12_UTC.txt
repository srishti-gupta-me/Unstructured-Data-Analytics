Photo by Keith Ladzinski @ladzinski | A Tuareg shepherd poses for a portrait at a local well in the heart of the Sahara, in Niger. The scarcity of water in the desert brings tribes and cultures together as they are drawn to areas where it can be found— ringing true the Tuareg proverb “Aman Imane," Water is Life.
