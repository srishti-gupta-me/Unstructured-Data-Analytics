Photo by @amvitale | Each day, eleven-year-old Irene, pictured with her mother, wakes at 6 a.m. so that she can gather firewood and water and help clean the house before walking three kilometers (2 mi) to her secondary school. She lives in a one-room shack with her parents and two siblings. Access to  education has profound impacts on everything from later wage earnings to infant mortality.
