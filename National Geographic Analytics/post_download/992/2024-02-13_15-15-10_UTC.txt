Photo by @orsolyahaarberg | After a long period of cold but fair weather, the arrival of a low-pressure system was accompanied by strong winds in Sweden's Stora Sjofallet National Park. The centuries-old Scotch pines, which had been groaning under the ever increasing burden of snow for weeks, had to withstand gales that stripped them of their thick ice shields in seconds.
