A new image from the James Webb Space Telescope has given scientists a stunning look at the dusty belts of debris surrounding Fomalhaut, one of the brightest stars in the night sky. Similar to the asteroid belt in our own solar system, these orbiting rings of shattered rock and ice can help reveal how planetary systems form.
