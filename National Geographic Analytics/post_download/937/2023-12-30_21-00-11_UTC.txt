Photo by Karine Aigner @kaigner | Sometimes, it’s not your best shot. The boat is writhing, the subject is on the move. But then right before your eyes, among the hyacinth leaves, the water explodes in a fury, followed by silence. A massive head rises out of the green. It looks out at its audience as if to say, Was the performance satisfactory? The audience of floating humans erupts in audible applause—sharing what we know was a moment of privilege awarded by the wild.
