Fashion designers and celebrities recently gathered in Paris for Fashion Week—but they weren’t the only ones making headlines. Bedbugs crawling through the cushioned crevices of the city flooded social media, fueling widespread concern due to the number of travelers that could return home with a bloodsucking hitchhiker in tow. The situation in Paris may not be an outbreak at all, but rather evidence of a longstanding issue and an example of what makes these critters so effective.
