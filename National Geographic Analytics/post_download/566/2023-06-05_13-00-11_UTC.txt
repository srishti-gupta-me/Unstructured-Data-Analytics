Photo by @paoloverzone | Conservators Zahraa Youssef Ahmed (at right) and Asmaa Ahmed document and archive singular artifacts found at the temple of Amenhotep III in Luxor, Egypt. They are part of a German Egyptian research team led by archaeologist Hourig Sourouzian. Built during the 18th dynasty, the funerary temple was a monument to Amenhotep III, a venue for the pharaoh’s worship and posthumous glorification.
