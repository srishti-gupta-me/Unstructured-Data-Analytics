The 2021 volcanic blast in the Canary Islands entombed more than 2,800 buildings in lava and ash. Crystallized bits of lava from the eruption contain details about what made La Palma’s first eruption in 50 years so powerful and destructive—and possible clues for scientists to help predict what types of eruptions will happen in the future.
