Photo by Keith Ladzinski @ladzinski | Vehicle lights outline Trail Ridge Road in Colorado's Rocky Mountain National Park, the highest-elevation continuous paved road in the United States, cresting at an impressive 12,183 feet (3,713 m). In the distance, Longs Peak is silhouetted by the glow of the Denver area. Roughly 4.3 million people visited the park in 2022, making it the 4th most visited U.S. national park.
