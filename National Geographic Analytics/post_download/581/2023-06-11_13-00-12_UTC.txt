Photo by @beverlyjoubert | Full bellies … and full of mischief. These muddy young cubs have a long way to go before they can lay claim to a kill of their own, so posing atop one will have to do for now. Botswana’s Okavango Delta certainly holds the promise of rich hunting grounds for these future apex predators—if we humans can safeguard this fragile and unique landscape.
