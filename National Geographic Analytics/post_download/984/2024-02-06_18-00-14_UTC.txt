ADHD diagnoses are climbing among adult women. Experts say it’s likely not a wave of misdiagnoses—but a sign that medicine is finally catching up to reality. The overwhelming majority of research on ADHD has been done in boys and men, but symptoms often present differently for girls and women, causing many to go untreated.
