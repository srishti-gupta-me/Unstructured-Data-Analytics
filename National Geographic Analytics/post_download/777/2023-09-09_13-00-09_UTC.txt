The name itself is both alarming and self-explanatory: the death cap mushroom. Unlike an edible mushroom—which they can be hard to distinguish from—death caps can cause extreme damage to the liver and kidneys, or in some cases, death. In fact, they're responsible for 90 percent of the mushroom-related fatalities that occur every year.
