Photo by Erika Larsen @erikalarsen888 | At Zoo Tampa, an all-female team led by Jaime Vaccaro treats injured and sick manatees—like this one—at the Manatee Critical Care Center, which has three 16,000-gallon medical treatment pools, two underwater pools, and a public observation area. Manatees are a keystone species whose behavior can alert us to environmental and habitat changes that affect our survival. These beautiful creatures are easy to love—attracting adults and kids alike—but recent die-offs show that if we are not alert, they can be gone in the blink of an eye.
