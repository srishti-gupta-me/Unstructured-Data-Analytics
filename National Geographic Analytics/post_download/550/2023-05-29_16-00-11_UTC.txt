Photo by @paoloverzone | In 1751 a meteorite landed in Hrašćina, Croatia, with witnesses reporting an explosion and a fireball in the sky—claims that were later dismissed as fairy tales. The largest chunk of that meteorite, weighing 88 pounds, is now displayed in the Natural History Museum Vienna.
