Photo by @rubensalgadoescudero | Cristobal Cespedes Lorenzo, 51, ferries coconuts across the river to his home in Copala, on the coast of Mexico's state of Guerrero. Cristobal and Francisco Manzanares Cagua (in the water), 16, sell their pickings to a company that makes coconut butter and oil.
